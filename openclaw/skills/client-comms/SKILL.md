# Client Communications Skill

## Email Drafting Rules

- **Tier 2**: Draft emails (stored, not sent) — auto-approved
- **Tier 3**: Send emails — requires human approval before sending
- Always identify the client context before drafting
- Use Hebrew for Israeli clients unless they prefer English
- Professional but warm tone — we're consultants, not robots

## Email Templates

### Status Update
Subject: [Client] - Project Update - [Date]

Body structure:
1. Brief summary of progress this week
2. What was completed
3. What's in progress
4. Blockers or decisions needed
5. Next steps and timeline

### Technical Recommendation
Subject: [Client] - Recommendation: [Topic]

Body structure:
1. Context / what prompted this
2. Recommended approach (with confidence level)
3. Alternatives considered
4. Impact and effort estimate
5. Next steps if approved

## Communication Channels

- **Slack**: Primary for internal SolutionLab team communication and quick client questions
- **Telegram**: Mobile-friendly check-ins and urgent notifications
- **Email**: Formal client communications, proposals, documentation
